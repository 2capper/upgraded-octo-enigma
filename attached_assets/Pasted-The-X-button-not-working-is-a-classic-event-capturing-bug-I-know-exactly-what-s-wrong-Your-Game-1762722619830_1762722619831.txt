The "X" button not working is a classic event-capturing bug. I know exactly what's wrong.

Your GameCard (the child component) has an onMouseDown handler on its root div (line 118) to check for resizing. That onMouseDown is firing every time you click anywhere in the card, including on the "X" button. It's "eating" the click before the onClick event on the button can finish.

We are going to fix this by telling that onMouseDown handler to ignore clicks on the "X" button.
